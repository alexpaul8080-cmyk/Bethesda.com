Our Mission

Committed to
Lasting Change

For over 9 years, Bethesda has been a shelter for over 300 people, giving all the care, love and dignity they deserve.

Our mission started as a care giving centre for the elderly but has now grown into a sanctuary where we also embrace children and adults with mental challenges. We offer love, care, acceptance and holistic support.

At Bethesda, we believe that every person — whether old or facing health issues or disabilities — deserves to live with respect, belonging and hope. Our home is more than just a shelter to our residents. It is like a mini hospital and rehabilitation centre, providing round-the-clock care.

Our Journey

Nine Years of Hope

2016
Founded
Foundation Established

Bethesda Old Age Home was founded with a vision to be "A Home of Hope and Compassion" by SM Prabha, drawing on her 25+ years as a nurse.

2018
Growth
Expanded Our Care

Grew our community to over 40 family members, welcoming more elderly residents and reaching more people than ever with our mission.

2020
Resilience
COVID-19 Response

Despite the pandemic, we continued serving daily meals and medical care — never leaving our residents behind, even while facing financial challenges.

2022

Mission Extension

Extended our mission to care not only for the elderly, but also for children and adults with mental disabilities, and began supporting 2 underprivileged schools.

Common Questions

Bethesda Home For Aged Trust is a registered non-profit organisation based in Bengaluru, Karnataka, founded in 2016 by SM Prabha — a nurse with over 25 years of experience. What began as a shelter for the elderly has grown into a full sanctuary that also cares for children and adults with mental challenges. We operate like a mini hospital and rehabilitation centre, providing round-the-clock medical care, therapies, counselling and emotional support. Over 9 years we have sheltered more than 300 residents and helped over 500 people, all while supporting 2 underprivileged schools in our community. We believe every person — regardless of age or ability — deserves to live with dignity, respect and hope.
