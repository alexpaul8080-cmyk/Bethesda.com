Our Story

Our Mission

At Bethesda, we believe that every life has value and every individual deserves the chance to live with dignity. Too many people are still struggling for the basics—food, education, and healthcare—that so many of us take for granted. With compassion at the core of our mission, we reach out to those in need, offering not just resources but also hope and encouragement.

Through our faith-driven programs, we walk alongside families, helping them rebuild their lives with strength and courage. Every meal shared, every child educated, and every life healed is a step toward a future where no one is forgotten. We are more than an organization—we are a family united by love, faith, and the belief that together, we can create lasting change.

Our Journey

2016

Foundation Established

Started as a small community initiative helping 5 families in our local area.

2018

Expansion of our community

We grew our community to over 40 family members, reaching more people than ever with our mission of hope and support.

2019

Education Initiative

Launched our flagship education program, funding 2 schools in underserved regions.

2020

COVID-19 Response

Provided emergency relief to over 23+ families affected by the pandemic.

Present

Ongoing Impact

Continuing our work across Karnataka & Kerala with a team of 60+ dedicated staff and volunteers.
